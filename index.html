About Attorney Dr. Rami Ibrahim Shabrawi

Dr. Rami Ibrahim Shabrawi is considered one of the most prominent lawyers in Jordan, as he has extensive experience spanning many years in the fields of civil, criminal, commercial, real estate, labor, insurance and arbitration law.

He worked on a number of complex cases that set important legal precedents, which earned him a distinguished reputation in the legal community.

Dr. Rami is distinguished by his meticulous professional approach to studying and analyzing cases, and his constant pursuit of achieving the best results for his clients within a sound legal framework that ensures justice and fairness.
